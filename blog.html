Five Reasons Why Every Small Business Owner Needs a Custom Website

March 19th, 2017

One of the biggest challenges for small business owners is attracting customers and gaining their purchase. However, you have access to a nearly unlimited pool of potential customers right at your fingertips. All you need to tap into this potential is an awesome website that gives viewers a great first impression of your business. Here are just a few of the reasons to get your brand online:

1. It’s cheaper in the long run
Anyone who has advertised with physical media knows how expensive it can be. Many people hesitate to have a custom website created because it can be pricey upfront. However, once it is created, a website is relatively cheap to maintain and will save you money on printed advertisements.

2. Your brand is accessible any time, day or night
Closing the doors of your business at night means no more customers. If someone needs information, or wants to purchase something, they are out of luck until you open the next day. The good news is, you never have to shut down your website. Potential customers will be able to access your business at any time without you needing to be there.

3. Get a snapshot of your customers
By harnessing the power of tools like Google Analytics, you can get an idea of the customers who visit your site. By getting a demographic of who uses your site, you can better market your products or services.

4. Gain credibility
Having a custom built, professional looking website, helps give customers a strong first impression of your business. They are much more likely to purchase something from a company who has a professional website than one that does not.

5. Keep things current and customers up to date
Websites are easy to maintain, meaning that you are able to update the content on your custom site whenever you want. This makes it easy for you to keep customers up to date on the latest products, sales, or news from your business.
